Airplane!

You're on a flight back home from Lollapazoola, when suddenly you hear the stewardess over the PA. "Is there a puzzler on board?"
After raising your hand you are quickly whisked to the cockpit and are given the low down from the pilots. The autopilot has been hacked.
You need to disable the 3 systems and help land the plane safely, otherwise you may not live to puzzle again.
